Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Plain English summary: We collect only what's needed to run the service. We never sell your data. We never use it for advertising. Your exports are always free. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

1. Who We Are

AmicaBull ("we", "us", "our") operates the co-parenting resolution platform at amicabull.com. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users, especially given the sensitive nature of co-parenting communications.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Name, email address, and password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Role (co-parent, attorney, mediator, therapist)
  • Children's first names and ages (for issue context only)

2.2 Communication Data

  • Issue submissions, responses, and rebuttals between co-parents
  • AI-generated mediation summaries and compromise options
  • Timestamps, read receipts, and SHA-256 integrity hashes
  • Flexibility scores and voting records

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device type, browser version, and IP address (for security only)
  • Usage analytics (anonymized, no personal identifiers)

2.4 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • Financial account details (payments processed by Stripe)
  • Location data (GPS check-ins on Professional plan are opt-in and stored separately)
  • Data from children directly — we never communicate with minors

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: Store and display your communications, generate AI mediation summaries, calculate flexibility scores
  • Security: Prevent unauthorized access, detect fraud, maintain tamper-proof audit trails
  • Improve the platform: Anonymized, aggregated analytics only — never individual data
  • Legal compliance: Respond to valid court orders or subpoenas

We NEVER use your data for: Advertising, data brokerage, user profiling, marketing to third parties, or training AI models on your personal communications.

4. AI Processing

Our AI mediation features process your communication text to generate neutral summaries and compromise options. This processing occurs through our Cloud Functions infrastructure using the Anthropic Claude API.

  • Your text is sent to the AI model only when mediation is requested
  • The AI provider (Anthropic) does not store or train on your data per our Data Processing Agreement
  • AI-generated content is always clearly labeled as such
  • No automated decisions are made — both parents must agree on any resolution

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted via TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: AES-256 encryption on all stored data
  • Authentication: Two-factor authentication available on all plans
  • Immutability: Messages cannot be edited or deleted after sending — this protects both parties
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) with SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Hash verification: Every message is SHA-256 hashed for tamper-proof audit trails

6. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Your co-parent: Only within the structured issue resolution flow
  • Invited professionals: Attorneys or mediators you explicitly grant access to (Professional plan)
  • Service providers: Firebase (hosting/database), Anthropic (AI processing), Stripe (payments) — all under Data Processing Agreements
  • Legal authorities: Only with valid court orders or subpoenas — we will notify you unless legally prohibited

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

AmicaBull does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We collect only children's first names and ages as provided by their parents, solely for the purpose of contextualizing co-parenting issues. We do not:

  • Create accounts for children
  • Communicate directly with children
  • Collect or store children's contact information
  • Share children's information with third parties

8. Your Rights

All Users

  • Access: Export all your data at any time (free on all plans)
  • Portability: Download in PDF, CSV, or JSON format
  • Deletion: Request complete account and data deletion at any time
  • Correction: Update your profile information

California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to know what data we collect and how it's used
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt out of data sales — we do not sell data, so this right is automatically satisfied
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

  • All rights above, plus right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority
  • Legal basis for processing: contract performance (providing the service) and legitimate interest (security)

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Free plan message history: 90 days of active display, but all data retained for legal export purposes
  • After account deletion: All personal data deleted within 30 days, except where legally required to retain (e.g., active court orders)
  • Backups: Encrypted backups retained for 90 days after deletion, then permanently destroyed

10. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

11. Important Legal Disclaimers

AmicaBull is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. AI-generated mediation summaries and compromise options are suggestions based on common co-parenting mediation practices. They are not legal recommendations. Always consult a qualified family law attorney for legal decisions affecting custody, parenting time, or child welfare.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@amicabull.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours for privacy requests
